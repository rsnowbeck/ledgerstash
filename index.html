What Is LedgerStash?

LedgerStash is a secure document exchange and PBC task management platform purpose-built for CPAs, accountants, and tax professionals. It replaces email attachments and shared drives with one organized, encrypted vault.

Key Features

  • Secure PBC List Management — Create and track "Provided By Client" task lists with due dates, priorities, and templates.
  • Bank-Grade Document Vault — AES-256 encrypted storage with per-client folders, file versioning, and full audit trails.
  • Busy Season Auto-Pilot — Automated email reminders chase missing documents on a configurable schedule.
  • Frictionless Client Experience — Clients receive a secure link to upload documents. No passwords, no app downloads.
  • White-Label Portal — Your firm's logo, brand colors, and custom sender name.
  • Team Collaboration — Invite team members with role-based access.
  • Audit-Ready Export — Download organized document packages with timestamps and activity history.

How It Works

  1. Add Your Clients — Enter client names and emails, or bulk import from CSV.
  2. Create PBC Lists — Define which documents you need (W-2s, 1099s, bank statements).
  3. Send Document Requests — Clients receive a branded email with a secure upload link.
  4. Track Submissions — Monitor who has submitted and who hasn't. Send reminders and export reports.
