What Is Ledger Stash?

Ledger Stash is a secure document exchange and PBC task management platform purpose-built for solo CPAs, boutique accounting firms, and corporate controllers. It replaces email attachments and shared drives with one organized, encrypted vault — with no per-user fees and no client accounts required.

Key Features

  • Secure PBC List Management — Create and track "Provided By Client" task lists with due dates, priorities, and templates for individual returns, W-2 collection, 1099 forms, and K-1 packages.
  • Bank-Grade Compliance Vault — AES-256 encrypted storage with per-client folders, file versioning, and full audit trails. IRS 4557, FTC Safeguards, and GLBA compliant.
  • Busy Season Auto-Pilot — Automated email reminders chase missing documents on a configurable schedule so you don't have to.
  • Frictionless Client Experience — Clients receive a secure magic link to upload documents. No passwords, no app downloads, no accounts to create.
  • White-Label Portal — Your firm's logo, brand colors, and custom sender name. Clients see your firm, not ours.
  • Engagement Letter Tracking — Send engagement letters, fee agreements, and authorization forms. Track who has signed and automate follow-ups for the rest.
  • ESIGN & UETA Compliant Audit Trail — Every acknowledgment captures IP address, timestamp, browser fingerprint, and the signer's typed name. Downloadable PDF certificates for legally defensible proof.
  • Audit-Ready Export — Download organized document packages with timestamps, IP tracking, and full activity history for any client or engagement.
  • Team Collaboration — Invite unlimited team members with role-based access at no extra cost.
  • Real-Time Firm Dashboard — See every client's completion status at a glance. Track who's uploaded, who hasn't, and what's overdue across your entire book.

How It Works

  1. Add Your Clients — Import your client list via CSV or add them individually. Each client gets their own secure vault.
  2. Create PBC Lists — Define which documents you need (W-2s, 1099s, bank statements). Use templates or build custom lists with due dates.
  3. Invite & Exchange — Clients receive a branded email with a secure magic link. They upload documents directly — no accounts needed.
  4. Track & Close — Monitor completion across your entire book. Auto-remind stragglers. Export audit-ready packages.
